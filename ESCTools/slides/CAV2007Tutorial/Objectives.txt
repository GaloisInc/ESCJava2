Objectives for the CAV 2007 tutorial

By the end of the tutorial attendees will:

0. Be able to explain the goals of the JML effort
   and how it relates to other work on formal verification.

1. Be able to read and write JML specifications
   using specifications that do and do not mention model features.

2. Be able to use the RAC, jmlunit, and ESC/Java2,
   and explain what each is good for.

3. Be able to explain the semantics of the most important JML features.

4. Know where to go for further information and help.
