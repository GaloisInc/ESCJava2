Objectives for the CAV 2007 tutorial

By the end of the tutorial attendees will:

0. Be able to explain the goals of the JML effort
   and how it relates to other work on formal specification and verification.

1. Be able to read and write simple JML specifications
   using specifications that do and do not mention model features.

2. Be able to explain the semantics of the most important JML features.

3. Be able to use the RAC and ESC/Java2 in the most basic ways.

4. Know where to go for further information and help.

What is omitted is:

- standard techniques for various common specification situations.

- use of jmlunit

- avoiding common traps and pitfalls.
